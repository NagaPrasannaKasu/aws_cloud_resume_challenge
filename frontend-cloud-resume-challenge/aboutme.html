About Me

As a QA engineer, I thrive on the challenge of ensuring software quality and reliability. My role involves meticulous testing and validation processes that guarantee products meet the highest standards before they reach the end user. I take pride in my attention to detail and my ability to identify potential issues early in the development cycle. My passion for quality assurance drives me to continuously learn and adapt, embracing new tools and methodologies that enhance my testing strategies.


In addition to my technical expertise, I am a cloud enthusiast . The rapid evolution of cloud technologies fascinates me, and I enjoy exploring how they can transform businesses and improve operational efficiency. I stay updated on the latest trends in cloud computing, from infrastructure as a service (IaaS) to platform as a service (PaaS), and I actively seek opportunities to integrate cloud solutions into my projects. This enthusiasm not only enhances my skill set but also allows me to contribute innovative ideas that leverage the power of the cloud.

My enthusiasm for technology and cloud computing inspired me to take on the Cloud Resume Challange.


Beyond my professional pursuits, I have a deep passion for Drawing, Photography and I love to film and edit the videos. These creative outlets provide me with a refreshing balance to my technical work. Drawing allows me to express my imagination and develop my artistic skills, while photography captures moments in time, telling stories through visuals. Whether it's sketching potraits or snapping candid shots of everyday life, these hobbies fuel my creativity and inspire me to see the world from different perspectives. Together, my professional skills and personal passions create a well-rounded individual who values both precision and creativity in all aspects of life.


I maintain a dedicated profile that showcases my artistic endeavors in both drawing and videography. This platform serves as a visual portfolio where I can share my unique style and creative vision.
